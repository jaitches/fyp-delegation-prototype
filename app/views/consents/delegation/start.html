Giving other people access to view your pensions

You can allow an authorised person to view your pensions:

  • a financial advisor
    • or
  • someone who gives guidance on behalf of the Money and Pensions Service

Financial advisors

You will only be able to select financial advisors who appear on the Financial Services Register.

Money and Pension Service guiders

Pension Wise is a free government service that offers free, impartial guidance. This service is provided by the Money and Pensions Service. To help the specialist understand your pension situation you can give them access to view your pensions.
